About AI Games

AI Games is a small, curated arcade from Planet AI, created by Pawan Maheshwari. Every title is a self-contained browser game — no downloads, no accounts. Open, play, learn.

The games turn real skills — prompting, AI ethics, machine learning, retail operations, compliance, business strategy — into short, playable challenges you can finish in a coffee break.

Our vision

The best way to learn a skill is to play it. We're building a growing arcade where serious workplace capability — rigor, compliance, judgment — is developed through joyful, game-shaped practice: built fast with AI, tested hard, and shared freely with learners and teams everywhere.

Privacy

This site sets no tracking cookies, runs no ads, and sells no data. Games run entirely in your browser; scores and progress stay on your device unless a game explicitly asks to share them (for example, QR multiplayer).

We follow privacy-by-design principles aligned with India's DPDP Act 2023. Questions? Reach out on LinkedIn.

Data & cookies

Cookies: AI Games sets no tracking or advertising cookies. There is no analytics script, no fingerprinting, and no third-party trackers on this site.

Local data: some games save scores or progress in your browser's local storage so you can pick up where you left off. This data never leaves your device, and you can clear it anytime from your browser settings.

Third parties: fonts are served by Google Fonts, and the site is hosted on GitHub Pages; each may log standard technical requests (like your IP address) to deliver the page. We receive none of this data.
