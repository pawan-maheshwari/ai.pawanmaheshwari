Executive User Guide HORIZON GenAI Strategy Simulator v1.0

Simulation Objective

You are the CEO of a large enterprise. Over 5 strategic phases you must transform your organization into an AI-native company. Balance five critical dimensions of success while managing risk, people, and competitive dynamics. Every decision carries trade-offs — just like real boardroom strategy.

How to Play

  1. Select your mode: Individual (solo mastery), vs AI (executive duel), or Hotseat (pass-the-device with a colleague).
  2. Customize your company name and industry. This slightly influences scenario flavor and starting conditions.
  3. Progress through 5 Phases. Each phase contains 3 high-stakes decision scenarios.
  4. Review the scenario context, then choose one of three strategic options. Preview approximate impact before confirming.
  5. Watch your metrics animate in real time. A radar chart shows your balanced scorecard.
  6. Occasional market events or internal alerts will appear — respond quickly.
  7. At the end of each phase, review outcomes and key leadership lessons.
  8. Reach the end with the strongest possible balanced scorecard to earn your AI Maturity rating and badges.

The 5 Executive Metrics

01 Projected ROI — Financial return from GenAI investments (higher is better)
02 Operational Efficiency — Productivity gains and process automation (higher is better)
03 Workforce Readiness — Adoption, upskilling, and cultural readiness (higher is better)
04 Risk & Governance — Responsible AI, compliance, bias mitigation, security (higher = stronger controls)
05 Strategic Position — Competitive advantage and market leadership through AI (higher is better)

Decision Framework

Assess → Understand data, talent, and tech readiness
Align → Secure board & C-suite coalition with clear vision
Pilot → Launch high-impact, low-regret use cases fast
Scale → Industrialize successful pilots across the enterprise
Govern → Embed responsible AI, measurement, and continuous learning

Pro Tips from Executive Programs

  • • Never let any single metric collapse — balanced transformation outperforms pure ROI plays.
  • • Workforce Readiness is often the hidden bottleneck. Invest early in change management.
  • • High Risk & Governance scores protect long-term value and avoid regulatory or reputational damage.
  • • In later phases, aggressive moves can backfire if foundation metrics are weak.
  • • Use the "Ask Advisor" button for strategic framing before committing to a decision.
