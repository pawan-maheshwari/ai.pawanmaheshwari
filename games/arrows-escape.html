How to play

Tap an arrow to launch it off the board in the way it points.

An arrow can only leave if its lane to the edge is empty. Tap one that's blocked and they collide — you lose a .

Clear every arrow to win. Par equals the arrow count — finish at par and you wasted no taps for 3 stars. No timer, no dead ends. Order is everything.
